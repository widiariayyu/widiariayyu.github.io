About Widiari.

I am a professional Software Quality Assurance who is passionate about finding bugs and errors which exists on software and ensures the final product observes the company’s quality standards.

I just believe that there is no perfect systems, those will need improvement. Being as a QA, maintain the quality is the vital role while development process. That is why I get excited when testing, exploring, breaking, and wohoo found bugs.

I am an organized person, I would like to arrange a plan or schedule before I start doing something. During development phase, being proactively communicate how much time it needs to verify software. I believe with communication and organizing timeline, we can prevent the “the last minute QA testing” which can impact to product quality. Organizing the test environment to make sure that avoidable environment delays will not affect the QA timeline.

Outside of work, I love to read books, watch films and dramas, manage my personal finances, travelling, and listening podcast.
